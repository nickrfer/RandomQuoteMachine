Sir William Wallace

A Guardian of Scotland

Some of his feats:

  • Carrying out many rebellions, such as the raid of Scone, alongside William the Hardy, Lord of Douglas and Andrew Moray.
  • Winning the battle at the bridge of Stirling, by letting the English force cross the bridge only to be crushed at the other end.
  • Being knighted in a ceremony, at the 'Kirk o' the Forest'.
  • Sacrificed himself in his fight for the independence of Scotland, therefore being recognized as a national hero.
